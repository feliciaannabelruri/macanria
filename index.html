04 — Our Story

A heritage tea house,
brewed for
the future.

At Macanria, we set out to revive the beauty of Chinese tea culture for a new generation. Every cup begins with premium heritage teas — aged oolongs, jasmine blossoms, Wuyi rock teas — paired with fresh homemade soy milk in place of dairy. Lighter on the body, extraordinary on the palate.
